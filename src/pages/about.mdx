## Who?
Hi!  I'm Dan Govea, an Austin based frontend developer and design system fan.  I've worked for Creative Center at Charles Schwab for the past infinity years.

## Wait - this isn't a static site?
You're right.  As I said, I'm a fan of design systems, accessibility and DRY principles.  So I figured why build another site that I will be slow to update when I can just create a storybook instance and show some of the things I've dabbled with recently.

## What's Storybook?
"Storybook is a frontend workshop for building UI components and pages in isolation." That's what it says on their website and I agree.  Learn more at [StorybookJS](https://storybook.js.org/).

## Shouldn't there be more examples of your work here?
I've worked at Charles Schwab for the past hundred years and most of my work is covered by a NDA.  And when I'm home, I prefer to hang out with my awesome wife and awesome kids.  That said, I will be adding to this site little by little until I can remove this paragraph from this page.
